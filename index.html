About us


A page to guide you through your interviews! We have all experienced cold sweats before our interviews. This page is an open book of interview experiences, that would help you tackle yours with ease and a lot less anxiety.


We are developers from IIT Patna. The aim of this page is to bring real life interview experiences for you all! It is an encyclopedia solely dedicated for the same



MISSION: Our mission is to bring the student community together from around the world to help out each other.


VISION: We aim on building a community, a chain of professional advocates as well as college students, gaining from the personal experiences of each other. Interviews are the ice breaker, and you've got to make a good first impression in the company. We here aim on better first experiences for more people.
